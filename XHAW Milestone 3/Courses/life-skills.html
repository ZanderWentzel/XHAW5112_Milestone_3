Life Skills (Six-Month Course)

The life skills course equips learners with essential everyday knowledge, including financial management, effective communication, time management, and confidence-building. These skills help individuals improve their quality of life and become more independent and productive.

Duration: 6 months

Outcome: Learners gain practical knowledge to improve their personal lives and increase job readiness.
