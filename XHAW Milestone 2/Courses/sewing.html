Sewing (Six-Month Course)

The sewing course trains learners in hand-sewing and machine-sewing techniques. Participants will create garments, repair clothing, and produce household items such as curtains and pillowcases. The course also introduces small business opportunities for those who wish to sell custom-made clothing or textile products.

Duration: 6 months

Outcome: Learners gain valuable skills in tailoring and garment repair that open doors to employment or entrepreneurship.
